About

I am a computer graphics researcher and software developer based in London. Focusing on developing efficient and scalable simulations of physical phenomena such as liquids and granular materials, I have worked with DNEG since 2014 developing a collection of simulation tools for large scale VFX with industry standard technologies such as OpenVDB and SideFX's Houdini. I am one of the authors of, and a continued contributor to, OpenVDB AX first open-sourced by DNEG in 2017 and received my engineering doctorate from the Centre for Digital Entertainment @ Bournemouth University in 2019.
